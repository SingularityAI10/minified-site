So what is the Learning Portal

The learning portal is a freedom centre. We believe that minds are like parachutes, they only function when they are open. Our aim is that education should teach us rather how to think, than what to think — rather improve our minds, so as to enable us to think for ourselves, than to load the memory with thoughts of other men. It is a rich library of resources from all over the world from various reputable sources and institutions.
